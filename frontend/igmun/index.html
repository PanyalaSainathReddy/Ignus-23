ABOUT US

IGNUS Model United Nations is a two-day simulation for high school and college students, held at Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur.

Delegates from various Indian regions engage in discussion and diplomatic exchanges to address the difficult issues affecting the globe today at IGMUN. Delegates will be a part of UN/Indian Parliament representatives at IGMUN.

Delegates will get to know about the workings of International/National politics and problem-solving,gain knowledge of the specifics of international negotiation and teamwork, adopt new viewpoints, and build thorough remedies on important international challenges by engaging in topics such as International/National security, human rights, and more.
